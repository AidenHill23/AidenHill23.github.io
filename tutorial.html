How to shoot a basketball

  1. Keep your feet no further than shoulder width apart, preferably closer together, and slightly staggered.
  2. Flex your knees slightly.
  3. Turn more in the air and jump forward, keeping your shoulders back, if you need to shoot a longer shot.
  4. Dip the ball down to your waist or thigh.
  5. Position your elbow so it's inline with your shoulder, not directly under the ball.
  6. Position your shooting hand so that your fingertips are perpendicular to the seams in the ball
  7. Straighten your knees and jump.
  8. Jump slightly forward when you shoot, making sure your shoulders lean back and are relaxed.
  9. Push the ball upward with your shooting hand.
  10. Release the ball.
  11. Follow Through!
